Easy Egg Noodles Recipe in 4 Simple Steps

Written by admin on December 24, 2020 in Delicious Recipes

Noodle recipes are always a quick and easy affair. All you need aside form the noodles are day-to-day vegetables from your pantry and Asian sauces. However, many people shy away from this meal option, as it is considered unhealthy and junk food. Not to worry though, as our egg noodle recipe is both healthy and satiating. Made with eggs, vegetables, sauces, seasonings and more eggs, this recipe is not an overkill, but a sumptuous meal. Check it out!

Course Main Dish
Cuisine Asian
Prep Time 5 minutes
Cook Time 15 minutes
Servings
people
Ingredients
  • 300 gm mushrooms sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 250 gm dried egg noodles
  • 1 head broccoli broken into small florets
  • 50 gm Green peas
  • 3 Eggs
  • Salt and pepper
Instructions
  1. As per the instruction on the pack, cook the egg noodles. Once done, strain them, sprinkle some oil and keep them aside.
  2. Take a wok and heat some oil in it on high flame. Add the mushrooms and fry them until soft. Then add salt and pepper and keep frying until they turn brown. Then add paprika and soy sauce. Stir the ingredients. In a separate pot, add some water and bring it to simmer. Then add the broccoli and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Lower the flame and set the vegetables to one side of the pan. Crack the eggs in the remaining space in the pan. Once they start to settle, scramble them. Let the eggs cook thoroughly.
  4. Add the egg noodles with more salt and pepper if required. Add the broccoli and stir the ingredients. Increase the flame and toss for a minute or two. Serve hot.
Recipe Notes

Recipe Tip

You can also add ginger-garlic paste for an extra kick of flavour.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the usual noodles for this recipe?

Yes. If the egg noodles are not available, then you can use any noodles of your choice. 

Can I add Vinegar and Chilli Sauce to this recipe?

The recipe includes paprika to make it spicy. Yet, if you want to increase the spice factor then you can add chilli sauce as required. You can also add Vinegar to this recipe.
